An universal icon

The yellow rubber duck has achieved an iconic status in Western pop culture and is often symbolically linked to bathing.

One duck to rule them all, one duck to find them, One duck to bring them all, and in the darkness bind them ...

Jimmy-Bob Tolkien

Toy, art & protest symbol Did you know that politicians are scared of giant rubber ducks?

The world's largest rubber duck was created by artist Florentijn Hofman in 2007, measuring 16.5 m × 20 m × 32 m and weighing about 600 kg. Since then, Hofman has had several giant ducks on display all over the world.

In 2013, China blocked searches for "big yellow duck" because Chinese activists were photo-shopping the Rubber Duck sculpture into the Tank Man photo of the Tiananmen Square Massacre.

DUCK FACT #27:

Rubber duck races, also known as derby duck races, have been used as a method of fundraising for organizations worldwide. People donate money to the organization by sponsoring a duck. At the end of the fundraising drive, all of the ducks are dumped into a waterway, with the first to float past the finish line winning a prize for its sponsor.
